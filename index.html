❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Ramzan Nigehban Program 2026?
PM Pakistan's initiative providing Rs.13,000 to 120K low-income families during Ramadan 2026 via NADRA records.

وزیراعظم پاکستان کا خصوصی رمضان ریلیف پیکج — 80 لاکھ غریب خاندانوں کو 13,000 روپے کی امداد۔

How is eligibility determined?
Eligibility is based on NADRA database records and government socio-economic surveys. Low-income registered families are automatically included.
How to check via 8171?
SMS your 13-digit CNIC to 9999 — completely free. You can also check instantly on this portal. Both give the same result.
Where do I collect my payment?
Visit the nearest HBL or designated bank branch with your original CNIC. No other documents required. Payment is Rs.13,000 cash.

قریبی HBL برانچ میں اصل CNIC لے کر جائیں۔ 13,000 روپے کیش فوراً ملیں گے۔

I'm not eligible — what can I do?
Call 8171 for assistance or visit your nearest NADRA office to update your registration details for future government programs.

8171 پر کال کریں یا قریبی NADRA آفس جائیں اور اپنی معلومات اپڈیٹ کرائیں۔
